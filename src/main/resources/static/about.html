Personal Bio

Hi there and welcome to tyu.space! My name is Thomas Yu and I am the proprietor of this fine establishment. This website serves as my personal test bench for web-related things and for showcasing personal projects. Head over to the void to check some of them out.

I am a software engineer by trade and have been working professionally since 2014 after completing my degree in Computer Engineering at Virginia Tech. I am a firm believer of lifelong learning, especially with all the rapid advancements in technology and engineering these days. In this environment, becoming stagnant is an element in the recipe for failure.

Besides software, I have a wide variety of interests. You can probably figure out what they are based on the various pieces of content on this site.
